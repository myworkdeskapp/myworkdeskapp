Roles & Career Levels
WorkDesk uses a two-axis model: Roles define what a user can do across the platform, while Career Levels indicate seniority within an employee's track.
🏷️ Organizational Roles
Role 1
Entry Level
Front-line staff. Can view and submit requests; modifications require supervisor approval.
Role 2
Supervisor
Approves requests from Entry Level staff. Limited edit access with manager oversight.
Role 3
Manager
Full team management. Can approve supervisor-escalated requests. Requires Senior Manager approval for own modifications.
Role 4
Senior Manager
Cross-team oversight. Approves Manager-level requests. Requires General Manager sign-off for system-level changes.
Role 5
General Manager
Organization-wide authority. Final approver within the org. Can delegate Admin functions.
Admin
Organization Admin
Manages all org settings, user access, and configurations within their organization.
★ Super
Super Admin
Platform-level access. Can create/modify/remove organizations and assign Org Admins. CEO-level platform control.

Access & Permissions
Permissions are derived from each employee's Role. All modifications flow through an approval chain based on the requester's role. Changes are logged as immutable footprints visible only to higher-access users.
ℹ️ Approval Chain
Entry Level → Supervisor → Manager → Senior Manager → General Manager → Admin → Super Admin. Each level can approve requests from the level directly below them. Managers and above require approval from their own next level for self-modifications.
⚠️ Attendance Records
AUX/attendance records are immutable once recorded. Entry Level & above may submit a Modification Request with a written explanation and supporting attachment. Requests require approval from the next-level manager before any correction is logged.

🏢 Our Purpose
WorkDesk is a Central WorkDesk platform for Employers and Employees — making work more sufficient, organized, and convenient. We provide a unified workspace for HRIS, payroll, attendance, talent management, communication, and analytics — all in one secure platform.
🔒 Security Features
How We Keep Your Data Safe
  • End-to-End Encryption: All data in transit is encrypted using TLS/HTTPS. Sensitive fields (passwords, tokens) are hashed and never stored in plain text.
  • Role-Based Access Control (RBAC): Each user is granted access only to the features and data relevant to their assigned role (Entry Level → Super Admin). No user can access data outside their permission scope.
  • Immutable Audit Trail: All significant data changes are recorded as tamper-proof audit logs, viewable by Manager-level users and above. These records cannot be edited or deleted.
  • Session Management: Sessions are authenticated using secure tokens. Idle sessions are automatically invalidated to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Cloudflare Security Headers: The platform is protected by security response headers (Content-Security-Policy, X-Frame-Options, X-Content-Type-Options, etc.) enforced at the edge via Cloudflare.
  • Input Sanitization: All user-supplied inputs are sanitized before processing or display to prevent injection and cross-site scripting (XSS) attacks.
  • Access Monitoring: All login attempts and administrative actions are logged and monitored by WorkDesk operations.
🗄️ Data Privacy & Storage
⚠️ Your Data Belongs to You
  • Secure Storage: All organizational and employee data are stored securely in Cloudflare-managed infrastructure under strict access controls. Data at rest is encrypted.
  • Monitored by WorkDesk: Data access and platform activity are continuously monitored by WorkDesk to detect unauthorized access, anomalies, or breaches.
  • No Data Selling: WorkDesk will never sell, trade, lease, or otherwise transfer your data or your employees' data to any third party for commercial purposes.
  • No Unauthorized Sharing: Personal and organizational data is not shared with external parties without explicit written consent of the Organization Admin, except where required by Philippine law or valid legal process.
  • Data Minimization: We collect only the data necessary for the platform to function. Users may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal data in accordance with applicable law.
  • Retention Policy: Data is retained for the duration of the organization's active subscription, plus a minimum retention period as required by the Data Privacy Act of 2012 and the Labor Code of the Philippines. Upon account termination, data deletion is performed securely.

⚖️ Legal Compliance
Aligned with Applicable Philippine Laws and Regulations
  • Republic Act No. 10173 — Data Privacy Act of 2012: WorkDesk operates as a Personal Information Controller (PIC) and adheres to the principles of transparency, legitimate purpose, and proportionality in processing personal data. We are committed to upholding the rights of data subjects including the right to be informed, right of access, right to object, right to erasure, and right to data portability.
  • National Privacy Commission (NPC) Rules: Our data protection practices are aligned with NPC guidelines, including mandatory breach notification and the appointment of a Data Protection Officer (DPO) for the organization.
  • Presidential Decree No. 442 — Labor Code of the Philippines: Payroll computations, leave entitlements, work hours, and employee records managed through WorkDesk are designed to support compliance with the Labor Code, including overtime rules, statutory benefits, and mandatory contributions.
  • Republic Act No. 8792 — Electronic Commerce Act of 2000: Electronic records and transactions conducted through WorkDesk are recognized as legally valid electronic documents under this Act.
  • Republic Act No. 11032 — Ease of Doing Business Act: WorkDesk supports digitalization and streamlining of HR and administrative processes consistent with the government's e-governance agenda.
  • Bureau of Internal Revenue (BIR) Regulations: Payroll tax withholding rates and VAT settings available in WorkDesk are configurable to align with current BIR withholding tax tables and VAT regulations.
  • SSS, PhilHealth, and Pag-IBIG (HDMF) Contribution Laws: Statutory contribution rates for SSS (RA 11199), PhilHealth (RA 7875 as amended), and Pag-IBIG (RA 9679) are pre-configured as default deductions in the Finance & Payroll settings and may be updated by the Organization Admin in accordance with the latest agency issuances.
